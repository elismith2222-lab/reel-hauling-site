WHO WE ARE

A Family Business Built on Hard Work

Reel Hauling LLC is a family-owned and operated heavy hauling company out of Denver, North Carolina. We started in 2025 with one goal: move heavy loads safely, on time, and treat every customer like a neighbor.

From excavators and bulldozers to forklifts, graders, and oversized loads, we specialize in lowboy and dump C&D trailer transport. When you call Reel Hauling, you're talking to the people who actually drive the truck — not a call center.

We're fully licensed, fully insured, and ready to roll across the Southeast.
